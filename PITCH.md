# PrepBuddy: Your Empathetic AI Study Companion

**Hack2Skill PromptWars Submission**

## The Problem
Academic stress is a silent epidemic. Millions of students struggle with anxiety, burnout, and feeling overwhelmed, yet lack immediate, judgment-free support. Traditional resources are often slow, inaccessible, or lack context regarding the modern student's daily pressures.

## The Solution: PrepBuddy
PrepBuddy is an AI-powered, empathetic study companion designed specifically to guide students through the emotional and logistical hurdles of academic life. It is not just an answering engine; it's a supportive guide.

### Why PrepBuddy Wins:

1. **Uncompromising Privacy (Zero-PII):** Students need a safe space. PrepBuddy operates entirely on local storage. No databases. No accounts. Total anonymity.
2. **Safety First Architecture:** Our hard-coded Risk Detection Engine intercepts crisis keywords *before* they ever reach the AI, ensuring users in severe distress receive immediate, verified emergency resources—eliminating the risk of AI hallucination in critical moments.
3. **Resilient "Always On" Support:** Built with a localized Fallback Engine, PrepBuddy guarantees a supportive response even if APIs fail or connections drop. The student is never left alone with an error message.
4. **Hyper-Optimized Prompts:** Our underlying prompts aren't generic. They are deeply engineered to enforce an empathetic, Socratic, and structured persona that genuinely helps students break down overwhelming tasks.

**PrepBuddy isn't just a hackathon project; it's a deployable, safe, and deeply necessary tool for student well-being.**
